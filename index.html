About us

Kuvela Velocity Screening is a service orientated company that started with the goal to manufacture woven wire screening surfaces for the South African, African and International Mining and Quarry Industry.

Kuvela Velocity Screening (Pty) Ltd. supplies a full and comprehensive range of woven wire screen media, injection moulded polyurethane panels and products, and both injection and compression moulded rubber panels and products to all industries that employ the process of separation operating in Africa, whilst exporting to numerous countries outside the continent
